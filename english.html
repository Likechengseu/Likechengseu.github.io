About Me

Associate researcher, PhD Tutor. In June 2009, May 2012, and December 2018, he obtained a bachelor's degree, a master's degree and a doctorate degree in engineering from Sichuan University, Xi'an Institute of Electronic Engineering (206 Weapons Institute), and Southeast University, respectively. From November 2016 to November 2017, he was a visiting scholar at the University of Alberta in Canada. From June 2012 to August 2013, he was engaged in the research and development of radar signal processing at No. 206 Institute of Weapons. In December 2018, he stayed at the school as a teacher of the School of Information Science and Engineering, Southeast University.

Research Interests
